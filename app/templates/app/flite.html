Flite

You might want to use the voices on your mobile applications, browser, or desktop. With Flite, a TTS engine built at CMU, this is possible.

Android

The first step is to install Flite TTS from Google Playstore

After installing this application, you will have to:
  • Enable Flite from "Text to Speech Settings" on your phone
  • Install voice data
  • Go to "Language" settings and select the voice that you want.
  • By clicking on "Listen to a sample" you can verify that Flite is working.

iOS

We do not have an iOS version of flite yet. It is coming soon.

Desktop OS

Flite works on Linux, Windows and Mac OS X. Follow the steps below to install it.
  1. Download and set up Festvox; Flite is one of the packages
  2. Download the voices of your choice or you can also download and use the voices on the fly.
    Downloading on the fly 
     ./bin/flite -voice http://festvox.org/flite/packed/flite-2.1/voices/cmu_us_aew.flitevox -f doc/alice
    Using a local flite voice 
    ./bin/flite -voice voices/cmu_us_ahw.flitevox -f doc/alice
    More usage instructions on desktop usage can be found on Flite GitHub page.
